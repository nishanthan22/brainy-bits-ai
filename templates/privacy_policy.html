Privacy Policy

Introduction

Brainy Bits is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our AI-driven classroom monitoring system and website.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, and any other details when you use our services or contact us. Additionally, our system may collect data related to student interactions within educational environments, including behavioral metrics and engagement levels.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to enhance our AI models, respond to inquiries, provide requested services, and communicate updates about our products. The data collected helps improve the accuracy and effectiveness of our monitoring systems, thereby supporting educational outcomes.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties, except to trusted third parties who assist us in providing our services, as long as they agree to keep this information confidential. This includes service providers for data analysis, cloud storage, and system maintenance.

Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information and data collected by our systems. These measures include encryption, access controls, and secure data storage practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@brainybits.com.
