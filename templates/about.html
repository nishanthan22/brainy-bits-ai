About Us

At Brainy Bits, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the classroom experience with our AI-based monitoring system. Our system helps teachers understand student behavior by analyzing head pose, emotion detection, and eye tracking in real-time. This allows us to gather valuable insights into student engagement, focus, and emotional states, which can be used to provide a personalized learning experience tailored to each student's needs.

We address the challenges faced by educators, such as obtaining valid feedback and reducing bias in student evaluations. Our software generates detailed reports, offering actionable insights for teachers to enhance their teaching methods and improve overall student performance. This system is particularly beneficial for schools, colleges, and universities aiming to optimize their educational processes and outcomes.

However, to access our services, schools need to obtain legal authorization from the Ministry of Education in their respective provinces. We are committed to working with educational institutions to ensure compliance with all necessary regulations and provide the best possible support for our users.
